1. Start work

Tap Start New Shift

Select work mode, Other Duty or Drive

Enter Started At time and location

Tap Save

On Duty countdown from 14 hrs begins

Next Break In countdown timer displayed

2. Take a break

Tap Rest break

End Break countdown from 30 mins begins

A Rest break must be >= 30 mins to be considered legal.
Otherwise it counts as work-time.

On Duty countdown continues

Tap Save

Resume Work In countdown timer displayed

3. Continue working

Tap Drive or Other Duty

Enter Started At time and location

Tap Save

On Duty countdown continues

Next Break In countdown timer displayed

4. Finish work

Tap Menu > End Shift

Enter Ended At time and location

Tap Save

On Duty countdown stops

5. Lock shift

Tap Review & Lock Shift

Last chance to make modifications to your Logbook

Tap Lock

OK confirms completion of your Shift

All TSL Holders of Vehicles driven are sent a copy of your logbook.

6. Done
