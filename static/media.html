ABOUT US

Logmate is a cloud-based drivers logbook software platform, which enables commercial drivers, & transport operators, to record & manage work time entries electronically.

The App is simple to use, works on most popular smart-phone devices, and pretty much removes all the hassles and helps drivers & transport operators make good decisions when it comes to driver fatigue.

Launched in June 2014, Logmate has just begun, but has aspirations to be be preferred Electronic Drivers Logbook solution in New Zealand within a very short period of time.

OUR MISSION

We build refreshingly simple software which the transport industry uses to do their bit to keep New Zealand's roads safe. Our mission is to make it easy for transport operators and commercial drivers to comply with the Land Transport Act 1998.

Our core values:

  • We listen to our customers & build what they want and need
  • Simple is always better
  • We have fun everyday

Logmate strives to deliver a cost-effective service which makes it affordable for all drivers & transport operators. We take pride in helping to reduce fatigue related deaths on our roads.
