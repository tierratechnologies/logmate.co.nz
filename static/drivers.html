Details for Drivers

Existing Driver: Looking for the Drivers Admin? SIGN IN HERE

New Drivers: It's easy to get up and running on Logmate. Just follow the 3 STEPS listed below and you'll be kissing paper logbooks good-bye forever, in no time!

1) DOWNLOAD APP

If you haven't done so already, you can download the Driver Logbook App for FREE from your phones AppStore

If your a Driver and you purchase your own logbooks then you get a FREE 3 week trial. Nice eh!

2) CREATE AN ACCOUNT

a) Complete the REGISTRATION form

You can register either directly from within the App OR via the DRIVER SIGNUP form.

b) CONFIRM YOUR ACCOUNT

After submitting your Registration details, look for a confirmation email with an ACTIVATION link.

Check your SPAM folder if it doesn't show up within 5 minutes.

3) SETUP YOUR LOGBOOK

a) Load a Vehicle

Now that your Account is active, you need to add at least 1 VEHICLE before you can start logging your work-time.

This can be done either via the App or from within the DRIVER ADMIN.

YOU'RE GOOD TO GO!

If you specify someone else as the OPERATOR of the vehicle, they will receive an email advising them that you are intending to use Logmate.
